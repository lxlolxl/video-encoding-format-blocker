# Disclaimer

Video Encoding Format Blocker is provided for user-controlled compatibility, power, thermal, and troubleshooting experiments.

This extension does not decode, transcode, download, decrypt, copy, redistribute, or modify video files. It changes how selected websites see browser media capability checks. Websites may still choose codecs through other player logic, DRM logic, cached player state, server-side experiments, or browser changes.

Using this extension may reduce video quality, disable HDR, disable high-efficiency streams, increase bandwidth usage, or cause some videos to fail to play. Users are responsible for complying with each website's terms of service and local laws.
